The Theraprism is a neutral zone located outside of time and space with the purpose of rehabilitation for deceased beings that are to be, eventually, reincarnated. Bill Cipher was sent here after making a deal with the Axolotl immediately following his death at the hands of Stan Pines.

**History**
The Theraprism is located in the neutral zone of Dimension #5150 and outside of time. Bill Cipher was sent there following the decision of the Axolotl, who described it as his "hardest trial." If Bill was to see it to the end, he may live again. Upon arrival, Bill is put into the Dimensional Tyrant Ward of the Maximum Security Wellness Center. There, he undergoes "indefinite karmic rehabilitation." He must attend mandatory therapy sessions with other beings in his same situation.

At some point, Bill used his therapeutic journal arts and crafts session to create a book to reach out to the living world and try to free himself. Eventually, he was caught, and it was confiscated. Bill, like other beings there, still remains undergoing rehabilitation, hoping he may one day be redeemed enough to live again.

Gravity Falls Master Index

## Universe Overview

Gravity Falls is a paranormal mystery town in Oregon filled with supernatural entities, cryptids, dimensional anomalies, hidden conspiracies, and ancient secrets. The story follows Dipper and Mabel Pines during the summer of 2012 as they uncover the mysteries surrounding the town and the interdimensional demon Bill Cipher.

Core themes:

- Mystery
 - Family
- Cosmic horror
- body horror
- Hidden knowledge
- Alternate dimensions
- Fate vs free will
- Supernatural conspiracies

Stanley Pines

Overview

Stanley Pines, commonly known as Grunkle Stan, is one of the central characters of Gravity Falls. He is the great-uncle and summer guardian of Dipper and Mabel Pines, the twin brother of Stanford Pines, and the former owner of the Mystery Shack.

For much of his life, Stan worked as a traveling con artist, developing exceptional survival skills, charisma, and business instincts. Although he initially appears greedy and selfish, he is ultimately revealed to be deeply loyal to his family.

For thirty years, Stan secretly worked to reactivate the Universe Portal and rescue his missing twin brother, Ford. His determination ultimately saved Ford and played a critical role in stopping Bill Cipher during Weirdmageddon.

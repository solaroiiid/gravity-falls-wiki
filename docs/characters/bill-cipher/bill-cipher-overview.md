Bill Cipher

Overview

Bill Cipher is the primary antagonist of Gravity Falls. He is an ancient interdimensional dream demon originating from the destroyed second-dimensional world of Euclydia. Bill seeks absolute freedom from rules, laws, and reality itself, believing that existence is an illusion meant to be broken apart and reshaped according to his whims.

For decades, Bill manipulated Stanford Pines into constructing an interdimensional portal that would allow the Nightmare Realm to merge with the physical universe. His plans eventually culminated in Weirdmageddon, a reality-warping apocalypse that nearly destroyed Gravity Falls.

Although Bill was apparently erased inside Stanley Pines' mind during the series finale, later material revealed that he survived and was placed within the Theraprism, where he is undergoing indefinite karmic rehabilitation.

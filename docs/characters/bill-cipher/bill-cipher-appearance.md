Bill Cipher - Appearance

Default Appearance

Bill Cipher appears as a yellow triangular being with a single large eye. His appearance resembles the Eye of Providence and imagery associated with pyramids and conspiracy theories.

Key features include:

- Yellow triangular body
- Single eye with slit pupil
- Black bow tie
- Floating black top hat
- Thin black arms and legs
- Occasional black cane

Bill typically levitates rather than walks.

Physical Characteristics

Bill is technically a two-dimensional being but can interact with three-dimensional space.

His size is highly variable and can range from hand-sized to towering over buildings.

The lower portion of his body contains brick-like markings resembling a pyramid.

Eye

Bill's eye serves as both his primary visual organ and one of his most expressive features.

The eye is also one of his most vulnerable areas.

Possession Appearance

When Bill possesses a host:

- Skin becomes pale
- Eyes turn yellow
- Pupils become slitted
- Voice changes to Bill's

This appearance is most famously seen during his possession of Dipper Pines.

Angered Form

When enraged:

- Body turns red and orange
- Eye darkens dramatically
- Voice becomes deeper
- Black features become white

This form appears during moments of extreme anger and violence.

Theraprism Appearance

Following his defeat and later rehabilitation, Bill is depicted with cracks and visible distortions throughout his body.

These fractures reflect the damage sustained during his destruction inside Stanley Pines' mind.

Symbolism

Bill's appearance is heavily associated with:

- The Eye of Providence
- Pyramids
- Secret societies
- Conspiracy theories
- Dimensional geometry

His triangular form reflects his origins as a being from a two-dimensional universe.

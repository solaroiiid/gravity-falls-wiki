Stanford Pines - History

Childhood

Ford was born in New Jersey alongside his identical twin brother Stanley Pines.

Despite their different personalities, the brothers were inseparable throughout childhood and dreamed of sailing the world aboard the Stan O' War.

Ford was frequently bullied because of his six fingers.

The Science Fair Disaster

Ford developed a perpetual motion machine that attracted attention from West Coast Tech.

When the machine failed during evaluation, Ford mistakenly believed Stan had deliberately sabotaged it.

The resulting conflict led to Stan being thrown out of the family home and permanently damaged their relationship.

Backupsmore University

Unable to attend West Coast Tech, Ford enrolled at Backupsmore University.

There he earned multiple degrees and befriended Fiddleford McGucket.

Arrival in Gravity Falls

Using a large research grant, Ford moved to Gravity Falls to investigate supernatural phenomena.

He eventually documented his discoveries in a series of journals.

Portal Project

Ford's search for answers ultimately led him to Bill Cipher and the construction of the interdimensional portal.

Thirty Years Lost

After being pushed through the portal, Ford spent three decades traveling across dimensions while attempting to find a way home.

Return

Stan successfully restored the portal and brought Ford back to Gravity Falls.

Weirdmageddon

Ford became one of the principal leaders opposing Bill Cipher during Weirdmageddon and helped orchestrate Bill's defeat.

After the Series

Following Weirdmageddon, Ford departed with Stan aboard the Stan O' War II to continue exploring the world's mysteries.

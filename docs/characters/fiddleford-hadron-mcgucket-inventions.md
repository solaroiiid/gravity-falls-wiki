Fiddleford Hadron McGucket - Inventions

Overview

Fiddleford McGucket is one of the most gifted inventors in Gravity Falls. His creations range from advanced computers and dimensional technology to bizarre robots built for personal reasons.

Many of the technological achievements associated with Gravity Falls were either designed or assisted by McGucket.

Major Inventions

Memory Gun

The Memory Gun is McGucket's most famous invention.

It allows memories to be selectively erased from a person's mind.

Originally created to remove traumatic memories, the device eventually became the foundation of the Blind Eye Society.

Repeated use of the Memory Gun on himself contributed directly to McGucket's mental decline.

Universe Portal Components

While Stanford Pines designed much of the theoretical framework, McGucket was responsible for much of the engineering and construction required to make the Universe Portal functional.

His technical expertise was essential to the project.

Early Personal Computers

Before moving to Gravity Falls, McGucket founded Fiddleford Computermajigs and developed advanced computer technology.

His inventions made him financially successful and established his reputation as a genius inventor.

Shacktron Contributions

During Weirdmageddon, McGucket helped transform the Mystery Shack into the Shacktron, a giant mechanical combat machine designed to fight Bill Cipher.

UFO Research Equipment

McGucket worked alongside Ford in studying technology recovered from the crashed alien spacecraft beneath Gravity Falls.

Many experimental devices were developed during this period.

Notable Robots

Gobblewonker

A massive mechanical sea monster created to attract attention and gain recognition from his estranged son.

The Gobblewonker successfully convinced many residents that a lake monster existed.

Pterodactyltron

A robotic pterodactyl built after the collapse of his marriage.

The invention became one of his most dangerous creations.

Shame Bot

A giant robot reportedly constructed after a friend failed to attend his retirement party.

The robot was designed specifically as an act of revenge.

Gideon-Bot Components

McGucket assisted Gideon Gleeful in constructing portions of the Gideon-Bot.

Mechanical Skills

McGucket is capable of:

- Robotics
- Computer engineering
- Dimensional engineering
- Mechanical repair
- Reverse engineering alien technology
- Artificial intelligence design

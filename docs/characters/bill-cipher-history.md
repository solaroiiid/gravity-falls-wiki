Bill Cipher - History

Origins in Euclydia

Bill Cipher originated in Euclydia, a two-dimensional universe populated by geometric beings. Unlike others in his dimension, Bill possessed an abnormal eye that allowed him to perceive the third dimension and concepts beyond his world's understanding.

At some point, Bill became obsessed with escaping Euclydia and reaching higher dimensions.

The Euclidian Massacre

Bill ultimately destroyed his home dimension in an event later referred to as the Euclidian Massacre. The destruction killed everyone he had ever known, including his parents. Later material suggests Bill suffers deep trauma and guilt regarding these events despite publicly denying it.

The Nightmare Realm

After Euclydia's destruction, Bill established himself within the Nightmare Realm, a chaotic interdimensional space existing between realities. Learning of a prophecy involving the third dimension, Bill began searching for ways to enter the physical universe.

Ancient Gravity Falls

For centuries Bill manipulated civilizations, shamans, explorers, cults, and inventors in attempts to create a portal into the physical world. Ancient inhabitants of Gravity Falls eventually developed a zodiac ritual capable of defeating him and left warnings behind.

Partnership with Stanford Pines

In the late twentieth century, Bill made contact with Stanford Pines and posed as a helpful muse. Through manipulation and deception, he convinced Ford to build an interdimensional portal beneath the future Mystery Shack.

When Ford eventually discovered Bill's true intentions, he shut down the portal and became Bill's enemy.

Dreamscaperers

Bill made his first direct appearance after being summoned by Gideon Gleeful. He entered Stanley Pines' mind and attempted to obtain the Mystery Shack deed but was ultimately thwarted by Dipper, Mabel, and Soos.

Sock Opera

Bill tricked Dipper into making a deal and temporarily possessed his body, becoming known to fans as "Bipper."

Weirdmageddon

After obtaining the interdimensional rift, Bill gained a physical form and initiated Weirdmageddon. During this event he transformed Gravity Falls into a reality-warping nightmare and ruled the town alongside the Henchmaniacs.

Defeat

Bill attempted to enter Ford's mind but was tricked into entering Stanley Pines' mind instead. Ford activated the Memory Gun, erasing Stan's memories and trapping Bill inside. Bill was shattered and seemingly destroyed.

Survival and the Theraprism

The Book of Bill later revealed that Bill survived by invoking the Axolotl. Rather than being permanently erased, he was sent to the Theraprism and placed into indefinite karmic rehabilitation.

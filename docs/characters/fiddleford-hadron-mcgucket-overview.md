Fiddleford Hadron McGucket

Overview

Fiddleford Hadron McGucket is one of the most important figures in the history of Gravity Falls. Although many residents know him only as the eccentric "Old Man McGucket," he was once a brilliant inventor, scientist, and research partner of Stanford Pines.

McGucket helped construct the Universe Portal and played a major role in investigating the supernatural phenomena of Gravity Falls. After witnessing horrifying events connected to Bill Cipher and the Nightmare Realm, he developed a memory-erasing device and repeatedly used it on himself. Years of self-inflicted memory loss gradually destroyed his sanity and transformed him into the town's local eccentric.

Following the events of Society of the Blind Eye and Weirdmageddon, McGucket recovered much of his memory, reconciled with Ford Pines, rebuilt his life, and eventually became one of the wealthiest residents in Gravity Falls.

Stanford Pines - Dimensional Travel

Portal Accident

Ford was thrown into the interdimensional portal during a fight with Stanley Pines.

Thirty Years Across the Multiverse

Ford spent approximately thirty years traveling through countless dimensions.

During this period he:

- Studied alien civilizations
- Encountered dangerous entities
- Became a multiversal outlaw
- Acquired advanced technology

Dimension 52

One notable location visited by Ford was Dimension 52.

There he met an Oracle who later implanted a metal plate in his head.

Interdimensional Reputation

Ford became known across multiple realities.

Many authorities considered him a criminal due to the acquisition of restricted technology and artifacts.

Equipment Acquired

Ford returned to Gravity Falls carrying:

- A laser weapon
- A magnet gun
- A quantum destabilizer
- Various interdimensional artifacts

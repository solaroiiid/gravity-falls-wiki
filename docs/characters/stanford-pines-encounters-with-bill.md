Stanford Pines - Encounters with Bill Cipher

First Contact

While investigating the origins of Gravity Falls' weirdness, Ford discovered ancient warnings referencing an all-knowing entity.

Ignoring those warnings, he summoned Bill Cipher.

Partnership

Bill presented himself as a supernatural muse.

Ford believed Bill was helping advance scientific understanding and accepted his guidance.

Manipulation

Over time, Bill gained Ford's trust and influenced the construction of the Universe Portal.

Ford grew increasingly dependent upon Bill's knowledge.

Discovery of the Truth

After Fiddleford witnessed the Nightmare Realm, Ford became suspicious.

Bill eventually revealed his true goal: merging dimensions and unleashing chaos upon reality.

The Rift

Ford spent years attempting to prevent Bill's return.

One result of the portal experiments was the creation of the interdimensional rift.

Weirdmageddon

Bill used the rift to begin Weirdmageddon.

Ford became one of Bill's primary targets due to his knowledge of the weirdness barrier.

Final Defiance

Even under torture, Ford refused to reveal the equation capable of disabling Gravity Falls' natural barrier.

His resistance played a crucial role in preventing Bill from conquering the world.

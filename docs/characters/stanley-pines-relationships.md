Stanley Pines - Relationships

Stanford Pines

Ford is Stan's twin brother and the most important relationship in his life.

The brothers experienced periods of friendship, conflict, separation, and reconciliation.

Stan ultimately devoted thirty years of his life to bringing Ford home.

Dipper Pines

Stan acts as a mentor and guardian to Dipper.

Although they occasionally clash, Stan respects Dipper's intelligence and determination.

Mabel Pines

Stan shares a particularly playful relationship with Mabel.

He often encourages her creativity and appreciates her positive outlook.

Soos Ramirez

Soos is one of Stan's most trusted friends and employees.

Over time, Stan comes to view Soos as part of his extended family.

Wendy Corduroy

Wendy works at the Mystery Shack and assists Stan with daily operations.

Stan trusts her abilities and often relies on her support.

Bill Cipher

Bill Cipher serves as Stan's ultimate enemy.

Bill manipulated Ford, threatened Stan's family, and nearly destroyed reality.

Stan ultimately outwitted Bill and played a key role in his defeat.

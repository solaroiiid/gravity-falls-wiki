Stanley Pines - History

Early Life

Stanley Pines grew up in New Jersey alongside his twin brother, Stanford Pines.

Although the brothers were extremely close as children, their relationship eventually became strained after a series of personal conflicts and misunderstandings.

Life as a Con Artist

After being forced out of his family home, Stan spent decades traveling across the country.

During this period he worked numerous jobs, developed countless aliases, and became an expert con artist and salesman.

These experiences shaped his resourcefulness and ability to survive under difficult circumstances.

Return to Gravity Falls

Years later, Ford contacted Stan for help regarding the dangerous portal hidden beneath Gravity Falls.

A confrontation resulted in Ford being pulled into the portal.

After Ford disappeared, Stan assumed his identity and began operating the Mystery Shack while secretly attempting to restore the portal.

The Mystery Shack Era

For thirty years, Stan managed the Mystery Shack while continuing his efforts to rescue Ford.

Although many residents viewed him as a greedy tourist-trap owner, he was secretly working toward bringing his brother home.

Ford's Return

Stan successfully restored the portal and brought Ford back from interdimensional exile.

The reunion was initially difficult due to decades of resentment and misunderstanding between the brothers.

Weirdmageddon

During Weirdmageddon, Stan joined the resistance against Bill Cipher.

When Bill attempted to gain access to Ford's mind, Stan switched places with Ford and tricked Bill into entering his own mind instead.

Ford then used the Memory Gun, erasing Stan's memories and destroying Bill.

After Weirdmageddon

Stan eventually recovered his memories with the help of his family.

Following the summer's events, he and Ford departed aboard the Stan O' War II to explore the world's mysteries together.

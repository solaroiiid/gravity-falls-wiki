## Overview

Mabel Pines is one of the main protagonists of Gravity Falls. She spends the summer in Gravity Falls, Oregon, with her twin brother Dipper Pines while staying with their great-uncle Stan at the Mystery Shack.

Mabel is known for her optimism, creativity, colorful sweaters, energetic personality, and strong belief in friendship. Although she approaches problems differently than Dipper, she frequently helps solve the supernatural mysteries surrounding Gravity Falls.

## Personality

Mabel Pines is cheerful, outgoing, imaginative, and compassionate. She enjoys making friends, creating crafts, organizing events, and helping others feel included.

## Family

– Dipper Pines

Dipper Pines is Mabel's twin brother. The siblings have different personalities but share a close relationship and regularly work together to overcome challenges.

– Stanley Pines

Stanley Pines, also known as Grunkle Stan, is Mabel's great-uncle and owner of the Mystery Shack.

– Stanford Pines

Stanford Pines is Mabel's great-uncle. Although Mabel and Ford often have different interests, they care deeply about one another.

## Friends and Allies

– Wendy Corduroy

Wendy is one of Mabel's close friends and frequently joins adventures involving supernatural events.

– Soos Ramirez

Soos is a trusted friend who works at the Mystery Shack and often assists Mabel and Dipper.

– Waddles

Waddles is Mabel's pet pig and one of her closest companions. She wins Waddles during a local fair event and becomes deeply attached to him.

## Major Story Elements

– Summer in Gravity Falls

Mabel experiences numerous supernatural adventures during her summer stay in Gravity Falls, encountering magical creatures, hidden mysteries, and strange phenomena.

– Friendship and Community

Many of Mabel's storylines focus on friendship, trust, personal growth, and maintaining connections with the people around her.

– Weirdmageddon

During Weirdmageddon, Mabel becomes an important part of the effort to stop Bill Cipher and restore reality.

## Overview

Dipper Pines is one of the main protagonists of Gravity Falls. He spends the summer in Gravity Falls, Oregon, with his twin sister Mabel Pines while staying with his great-uncle Stan at the Mystery Shack. Dipper is intelligent, curious, cautious, and highly interested in solving supernatural mysteries.

Dipper earned his nickname from the birthmark on his forehead, which resembles the Big Dipper constellation.

## Personality

Dipper Pines is analytical, skeptical, and determined. He prefers logic and investigation when facing unusual situations. Although he is often more serious than his sister Mabel, he deeply cares about his friends and family.

## Family

– Mabel Pines

Mabel Pines is Dipper's twin sister. Despite frequent disagreements, the twins share a close bond and often work together to solve mysteries.

– Stanley Pines

Stanley Pines, also known as Grunkle Stan, is Dipper's great-uncle and owner of the Mystery Shack.

– Stanford Pines

Stanford Pines, often called Ford, is Dipper's great-uncle and the author of the mysterious journals. Dipper greatly admires Ford's scientific knowledge and investigative abilities.

## Friends and Allies

– Wendy Corduroy

Wendy is one of Dipper's closest friends. Dipper initially develops a crush on her, but they eventually maintain a strong friendship.

– Soos Ramirez

Soos is a loyal friend who works at the Mystery Shack and frequently assists Dipper during supernatural investigations.

## Major Story Elements

– The Journals

Dipper discovers Journal 3, one of several mysterious journals documenting the supernatural phenomena of Gravity Falls. The journal becomes one of his most important tools.

– Mystery Investigation

Throughout the series, Dipper investigates paranormal creatures, hidden conspiracies, cryptids, magical artifacts, and interdimensional threats.

– Weirdmageddon

During the event known as Weirdmageddon, Dipper helps lead resistance efforts against Bill Cipher and works alongside his family and friends to save Gravity Falls.

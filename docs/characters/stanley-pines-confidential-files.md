Stanley Pines - Confidential Files

Overview

The following records contain embarrassing, private, criminal, or otherwise undisclosed information regarding Stanley Pines. Most entries originate from unofficial reports, personal fears, hidden records, or information that Stan would strongly prefer remain secret.

Secret Shames

Wendy's Arm Wrestling Victories

Wendy Corduroy defeated Stan in arm wrestling three consecutive times.

Stan reportedly granted Wendy permanent immunity from overtime work in exchange for her silence.

Fake Funeral Failure

At one point Stan staged his own funeral.

Attendance consisted only of:

- Caryn Pines
- An IRS agent

The IRS agent allegedly leaned into the coffin and whispered:

«"This isn't over."»

Hunky Drifters Catalogue

In 1975, Stan accepted a modeling job for a publication known as the "Hunky Drifters Catalogue."

According to Stan, the assignment turned out to be considerably less respectable than the advertisement suggested.

Fussy Boy Commercial

Stan once served as the mascot baby for a diaper rash commercial.

He insists the footage actually depicts Ford.

Romantic Fiction Author

«The Duke's Temptations at Oglebottom Estate»

Personal Fears

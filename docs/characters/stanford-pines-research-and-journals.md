Stanford Pines - Research and Journals

Journal Project

Ford created three journals documenting the mysteries of Gravity Falls.

These journals became some of the most important artifacts in the series.

Areas of Study

Ford researched:

- Cryptids
- Dimensional anomalies
- UFOs
- Magic
- Time anomalies
- Bill Cipher
- Gravity Falls' weirdness field

Major Creations

Project Mentem

A device capable of encrypting thoughts and protecting minds from Bill Cipher.

Electron Carpet

A carpet capable of swapping minds between individuals.

Mind-Control Tie

A tie designed to influence the actions of others.

Containment Systems

Ford developed numerous devices designed to contain dangerous supernatural entities.

Legacy

Ford's journals became the foundation for Dipper's investigations and remain the most complete record of Gravity Falls' supernatural history.

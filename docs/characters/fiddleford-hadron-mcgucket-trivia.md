Fiddleford Hadron McGucket - Trivia

Name Origin

McGucket's middle name, Hadron, is a reference to hadrons, a category of subatomic particles studied in physics.

Hidden Knowledge

Throughout Gravity Falls, numerous cryptograms and hidden messages reference McGucket.

Several codes imply that he possesses important knowledge about the mysteries of the town long before the audience learns the truth.

The Scholar Symbol

McGucket represents the glasses symbol on Bill Cipher's Zodiac.

This symbol represents knowledge, intelligence, and scholarship.

Strange Eating Habits

McGucket is known for consuming unusual objects and substances.

Examples include:

- Books
- Novelty checks
- Various junkyard items
- A baby pterodactyl

He also attempted to eat Soos while Soos occupied Waddles' body.

Invented Vocabulary

McGucket frequently creates nonsense words and expressions.

Examples include:

- Scrabdoodle
- Thingamadiculous
- Robomajig
- Shim-sham
- Donkey spittle

These speech patterns became one of his defining traits.

Mental Condition

McGucket displays symptoms similar to jargon aphasia, a condition involving the accidental use of invented words and nonsensical phrases.

Within the story, however, much of this behavior is linked to repeated memory erasure.

President McGucket

Alex Hirsch jokingly stated that McGucket eventually became the 45th President of the United States and invented a vaccine using gadgets and inventions.

Favorite Activities

McGucket enjoys:

- Jigging
- Playing the banjo
- Musical spoons
- Sev'ral Timez
- Inventing machines

Fun Facts

- He can apparently smell money.
- He stole his hat from a scarecrow.
- He kept a pet alligator.
- He founded the Blind Eye Society.
- He helped build the Universe Portal.
- He ultimately became one of Gravity Falls' wealthiest residents.

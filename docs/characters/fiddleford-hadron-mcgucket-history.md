Fiddleford Hadron McGucket - History

Early Life

Fiddleford McGucket grew up on a hog farm in Tennessee. Despite humble beginnings, he displayed exceptional intelligence and eventually attended Backupsmore University.

While attending university, he became close friends with Stanford Pines.

Computermajigs

After graduation, McGucket founded Fiddleford Computermajigs in Palo Alto, California. His success as an inventor established him as one of the most talented engineers of his generation.

Partnership with Stanford Pines

Ford later recruited McGucket to assist with research into the strange phenomena of Gravity Falls and the construction of the Universe Portal.

McGucket became Ford's primary engineering partner and helped design much of the machinery connected to the project.

Encounters with the Supernatural

As research continued, McGucket experienced increasingly disturbing encounters with strange creatures and unexplained phenomena.

One encounter with a Gremloblin exposed him to his deepest fears and left him emotionally traumatized.

Creation of the Memory Gun

To eliminate traumatic memories, McGucket invented the Memory Gun.

Although effective, repeated use eventually became destructive.

The Portal Incident

During the first portal test, McGucket was briefly pulled into the portal and witnessed terrifying visions of the Nightmare Realm and Bill Cipher.

The experience convinced him that the portal would eventually cause an apocalypse.

He abandoned the project shortly afterward.

The Blind Eye Society

Haunted by fear and paranoia, McGucket founded the Blind Eye Society.

The organization's purpose was to erase painful memories from the residents of Gravity Falls.

Over time, McGucket repeatedly used the device on himself, erasing larger portions of his own memory.

Descent into Madness

Years of memory erasure gradually destroyed McGucket's identity and mental stability.

He became known throughout Gravity Falls as Old Man McGucket, the local eccentric who lived in the junkyard and built bizarre inventions.

Society of the Blind Eye

Dipper, Mabel, Wendy, and Soos eventually uncovered McGucket's past.

After recovering his memories, McGucket finally understood what had happened to him and began rebuilding his life.

Weirdmageddon

During Weirdmageddon, McGucket became one of the leaders of the resistance against Bill Cipher.

He helped construct the Shacktron and participated directly in the rescue of Ford Pines.

After Weirdmageddon

Following Bill's defeat, McGucket reconciled with Ford, regained much of his sanity, became wealthy through his inventions, and purchased Northwest Manor.

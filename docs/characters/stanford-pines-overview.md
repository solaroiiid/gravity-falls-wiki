Stanford Pines

Overview

Stanford Filbrick Pines, commonly known as Ford Pines or Grunkle Ford, is a scientist, paranormal investigator, and author of the three journals documenting the supernatural phenomena of Gravity Falls.

Ford spent years researching the mysteries of Gravity Falls before being manipulated by Bill Cipher into constructing an interdimensional portal. After discovering Bill's true intentions, Ford attempted to stop the project but was accidentally thrown into the portal and stranded across the multiverse for thirty years.

Following his return, Ford became one of Bill Cipher's greatest enemies and played a major role in defeating him during Weirdmageddon.
